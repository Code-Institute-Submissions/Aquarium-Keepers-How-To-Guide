About Us

After years of being involved in the Fish Keeping hobby, we've decided to take the next step by giving back to the hobby and the community! With all the knowledge we've gained over the years from research and personal experience alike, we offer support and guidance for anyone and everyone who needs help with the basics of Fish Keeping. Originally founded in a family home in the UK, our expert team is now comprised of many seasoned Fish Keepers from all over the UK! Any questions, please feel free to get in touch and one of our team will give advice to any problems you may have or situations you may find yourself in!
